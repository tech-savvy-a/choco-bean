About The Choco Beans

Founded by Anaya Hathaway in the year 2015. Over the 5 years, we started off as just a little bakery around the block. From a delicious freshly baked breads to cakes and cookies.

When our bakery was loved by everyone in town, we built our branches across the city and started an online delivery service.

Soon after, we decided to make customised chocolates for everyone to enjoy. Currently we serve vegan, absolute sugar free, healthy (yes you read that right!) and all other kinds of treats for everyone to savour and enjoy.
